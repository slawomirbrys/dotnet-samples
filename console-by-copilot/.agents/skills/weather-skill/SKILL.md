# Weather Skill

Gives real-time weather information for a specified location, including temperature, humidity, and weather conditions.

## Usage

To use the weather skill, simply ask for the weather in a specific location. For example:
- "What's the weather like in New York?"
- "Give me the current weather in London."
- "How's the weather in Tokyo today?"
The skill will respond with the current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, and a brief description of the weather (e.g., sunny, cloudy, rainy).

## Example Response

When you ask for the weather, you might receive a response like this:
"The current weather in New York is 75°F with clear skies. Humidity is at 60%."

## Supported Locations

The weather skill can provide information for most cities around the world. Just specify the city name, and the skill will return the current weather conditions for that location. 

## Data Source

The weather information is sourced from a reliable weather API, ensuring that you receive accurate and up-to-date weather data for your specified location.

## Limitations
- The skill may not provide weather information for very small or remote locations. 
- The accuracy of the weather information depends on the data provided by the weather API, which may occasionally have delays or inaccuracies.

## Conclusion

The weather skill is a convenient tool for quickly obtaining current weather information for any city. Whether you're planning your day or just curious about the weather, this skill can provide you with the information you need in a simple and efficient manner.
